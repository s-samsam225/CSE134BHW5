Overview & Functionality

My postpartum mental health app addresses the care gap that often occurs after hospital discharge, when new mothers face emotional and physical risks while caregivers experience overload. The goal is to reduce hospital readmissions, improve maternal mental health, and enhance patient satisfaction through continuous, accessible support. This solution is a GenAI-powered chatbot that provides personalized wellness tracking, appointment scheduling, and access to community forums. It includes a caregiver or partner mode, multilingual support, and a calming, inclusive design to reduce cognitive overload. The system is HIPAA-compliant, ensures sensitive data handling and informed consent, and can be integrated easily into existing telehealth platforms. While not a substitute for clinical care, it complements healthcare teams by improving patient trust, engagement, and overall well-being across diverse postpartum experiences.
